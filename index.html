From Trauma to Whole-Hearted Living

A Journey of Self-Discovery from behind bars

My Mission

From behind bars I have embarked on a journey to better understand myself and the actions that landed me in prison. It turns out that trauma early in life is what shapes our ability to manage our emotions, and connect with others. My goal is to share parts of my story to show that healing is absolutely possible once we are able to better understand exactly what trauma is, and how it affects the mind, body, and soul.
